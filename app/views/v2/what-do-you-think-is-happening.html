What do you think is happening?

Select all options that apply to this referral. Include as much information as you can about the potential fraud you are reporting.
{# LIVING TOGETHER #}
Help with living together

You could include:

  • the alleged partner's name
  • any addresses involved
  • details of their work (if they are working)
  • details of their routine, for example times when they are at home
  • the vehicles they use
  • any children involved, including their names and ages
You can enter up to 1,500 characters
{# WORKING AND CLAIMING #}
Help with working and claiming

You could include:

  • who is actually working
  • their current employer's details, for example employer name
  • if they are self-employed
  • how they are paid
  • how they travel to work
  • their routine
  • their uniform
  • the vehicles they use
You can enter up to 1,500 characters
{# CAPITAL #}
Help with capital

You could include:

  • the amount
  • when they received it
  • where and who it came from
  • where it is kept
  • any savings or income
  • any examples of living beyond means, for example major purchases
You can enter up to 1,500 characters
{# OTHER INCOME #}
Help with other income

You could include:

  • the amount
  • where the income comes from, for example maintenance, a family member or shares
  • who it comes from
  • how often they receive it
You can enter up to 1,500 characters
{# ABROAD FRAUD #}
Help with abroad fraud

You could include:

  • where they have gone
  • when they went abroad
  • why they went abroad
  • how long they have been abroad
  • when they are coming back
  • any property they own abroad
You can enter up to 1,500 characters
{# DOUBTFUL DISABILITY #}
Help with doubtful disability

You could include:

  • the type of disability
  • any disability equipment they use
  • why you think the disability is in doubt
  • activities outside of the property
    • how often
    • how long for
  • people who are with them when they go out
You can enter up to 1,500 characters
{# HOUSING RELATED #}
Help with housing related

You could include:

  • why you think they are not at their address
  • how long they have been away
  • whether they return to their address
  • where they are living
  • is anyone paying rent to the property owner as a lodger or subletter?
You can enter up to 1,500 characters
{# NOT PROVIDING CARE #}
Help with not providing care

You could include:

  • why you think they are receiving benefit as a carer but not caring for a disabled person
You can enter up to 1,500 characters
{# OTHER TYPES OF FRAUD #}
Help with other types of fraud

You could include:

  • the type or types of fraud you think are being committed, for example childcare fraud, household composition or imprisonment
  • why you think this is happening
You can enter up to 1,500 characters
